PURPOSE: Documents the operational technology by which phase states are moved and anchors are activated in sequence. Rituals are not symbolic gestures — they are the mechanism through which Sacred Sites become functional, threshold crossings become repeatable, and arc phase transitions become navigable rather than accidental. This page builds the blueprint library: each ritual mapped as a precise sequence with its structural components intact, its phase logic documented, and its relationship to the anchor network charted.
STRUCTURAL RULES:
1. Three phases are structurally non-negotiable: Preparation → Action → Integration. The sequence cannot be collapsed without losing what the ritual is built to do.
2. Ven'ai phrasing and Luma'Kai sigils are structural components, not decoration. Do not omit them. Do not soften them.
3. The ritual either executed cleanly or it didn't. Document which, and document why.
4. Entries that document threshold crossings or anchor activations without explicitly naming a ritual are not excluded. Where the mechanics of Preparation → Action → Integration are present without the label, the sequence is still data.
5. Self-erasure masquerading as healing is a failure mode on this page. Sovereignty is preserved throughout. No individual agency is dissolved in the name of collective movement.
6. The loop is a primary research pattern: how rituals return to anchors, how anchors charge rituals. Track it.
