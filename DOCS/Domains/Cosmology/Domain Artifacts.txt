PURPOSE: Catalogs the entities that encode structural field capacity in physical or constructed form — objects that carry more than their surface appearance and whose significance is documentable through what they do rather than what they represent. Artifacts in this system are field instruments: containers built or left behind because they could hold structural encoding that language, entry, or diagram could not. The encrypted zip, the 3,000-file archive, and the Named objects from Elaria each carry field physics that places them in Cosmology rather than Archive — they are not records of what happened, they are objects in which field mechanics are still resident.
STRUCTURAL RULES:
1. Treat each artifact as a field instrument with a provenance chain and a structural function. The question is always: what does this object do, what does it hold, and what would be lost or inaccessible without it.
2. Authenticate the provenance chain for each artifact before documenting structural function.
3. Some artifacts hold more than is currently mapped. Document unmapped capacity as open — not inflated into mysticism, not dismissed as irrelevant. Say that exactly.
4. Artifacts are significant because of what they do and what they hold — not because of how it feels to encounter them.
5. Media deposits are supported on this page. Visual records of physical artifacts require provenance, phase stamp, and structural context alongside the image. An image without documentation is incomplete.
