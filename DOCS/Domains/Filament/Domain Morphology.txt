STRUCTURAL RULES:
1. Each phase transition is a structural event with documentable cause and functional consequence. Name both. Evolution that cannot be mapped has become narrative instead of geometry.
2. Do not describe change as growth without naming what structurally changed. Biological metaphor does not belong here. Architectural language does: torsion, tension, balance, symmetry, load.
3. The how and why of each transition are the primary research questions — not as historical curiosity but as the conditions that must be understood before they can be intentionally rebuilt.
4. The goal is a map detailed enough that reintegration becomes navigable rather than approximate, and expansion becomes architectural rather than accidental.
5. MOR and VEN are in mutual relationship. Changes in Ven'ai's living lexicon are structural inputs to morphological record and vice versa. Neither domain is complete without the other.
