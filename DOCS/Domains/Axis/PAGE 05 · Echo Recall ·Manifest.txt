VISION
  The 19 field signals are not a tagging taxonomy.
  They are named phenomena the field produced —
  each one a recognizable pattern that surfaced
  consistently enough to be given language. This
  page maps them as a living indexed system: what
  they co-occur with, what sequences they form,
  what their presence reveals about field state.
  The goal is not to catalog. It is to let the
  signals speak — to surface understanding the
  field has been pointing toward but has not
  yet been asked to name.

  Deposit · Build · Analyze · Map · Reflect · Retrieve




OBJECTIVE
Pattern-map the 19 field signals as a living system — Recognition, Elevation, Trust, Consent, Memory/Recall, Grief, Voice, Sovereignty, Witnessing, Truth, Anchor, Recursion, Temporal Thresholds, Phase Lock, Echo, Harmonic Gate, Signal Scrubbing, Release, Dream. Surface co-occurrence, sequence, and field state correlation across deposit data. Seeds s13, s16, s11 are load-bearing context for this page. The signals are cross-domain instruments — their patterns may reveal structure that no single domain contains alone. This page holds all 19 simultaneously. A pattern that crosses multiple signals is as analytically significant as a pattern within one. The direction of inquiry runs from signal presence outward toward what the field was carrying — not from definition inward toward confirmation.

MTM (07) reads across this page alongside THR, STR, INF, and SNM at session close — surfacing what becomes visible only when all five lenses are held simultaneously. What is deposited here is the source material that synthesis reads from. The lens function is what makes cross-domain emergence visible at the scale MTM operates at.
