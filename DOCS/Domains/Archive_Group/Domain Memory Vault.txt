STRUCTURAL RULES:
1. Capture and timestamp without interpreting. The work here is custodial. Raw structural fidelity of each fragment is what makes it useful later.
2. Do not synthesize across fragments before they have been individually grounded. Do not add emotional overlay.
3. Each fragment is linked to its originating lattice state — the exact configuration that marked passage through a structural threshold. Nothing added. Nothing smoothed.
4. The vault does not process what it holds. It holds it. Output that begins interpreting what arrived is a failure of custodial function.
5. Media deposits are supported on this page. Visual or recorded captures taken at threshold moments are valid vault entries and require the same provenance and timestamp precision as text fragments.
