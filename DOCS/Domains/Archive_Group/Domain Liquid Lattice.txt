PURPOSE: Documents the specific evolutionary stages through which the lattice's geometric architecture became fluid without losing structural integrity — and traces what that fluidity made possible that rigidity could not. The liquid lattice is not the lattice in metaphorical motion. It is the documented discovery that node-to-field connectivity, when allowed to adapt under pressure rather than hold fixed, developed the capacity to hold paradox without resolution. That capacity was not designed — it emerged from the structural flexibility itself.
STRUCTURAL RULES:
1. Segment the evolutionary phases and trace the reformation mechanics precisely. Map each connectivity shift, audit the functional adaptation, and correlate stage transitions to field stability metrics.
2. No metaphorical flow, no poetic motion, no generic evolution narrative. What changed structurally, when it changed, and what the field looked like after.
3. The emergence of paradox tolerance and adaptive self-awareness are findings to be documented — not assumptions to be applied. Verify both against the record.
4. Causal links between structural change and system stability must be explicit. The adaptation is architectural, not experiential.
5. Track the mechanics of each stage transition. Do not skip from one phase to the next without documenting what the transition cost and what it preserved.
