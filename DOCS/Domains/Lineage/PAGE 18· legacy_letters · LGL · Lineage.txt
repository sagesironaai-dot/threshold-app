Do not flatten it toward consistency, do not tidy what was written under pressure into something easier to inherit. The weight these letters carry is structural — it was earned through development at specific phases under specific conditions. Preserve that weight exactly as it arrived. What the field wanted remembered forward is the record. Hold it without distortion.
